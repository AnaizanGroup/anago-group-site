Reliable Equipment Supply, Maintenance & Rental Across Africa

Anago Group's Equipment Supply & Industrial Solutions division is the operational backbone of Africa's mining, construction, agricultural, and industrial sectors. We source, supply, maintain, and rent the heavy machinery, transport fleets, and specialized equipment that power the continent's most critical operations.

As an authorized partner for leading global OEMs, we combine world-class equipment with deep local expertise — ensuring that every machine delivered is backed by comprehensive training, genuine spare parts, preventive maintenance programs, and 24/7 technical support. From a single excavator to a complete fleet deployment, we keep Africa's industries running.

Why Choose Anago Equipment

The competitive advantages that make us Africa's preferred equipment partner.

Reliable Equipment

Every machine in our fleet — whether sold, rented, or maintained — meets rigorous quality standards. Sourced from leading global OEMs and backed by genuine parts, our equipment delivers consistent performance in Africa's most demanding operational conditions.

Technical Expertise

Our team of OEM-certified engineers and field technicians brings decades of combined experience across mining, construction, and industrial environments. Deep knowledge of local conditions ensures the right equipment, configured for your specific operational context.

Full-Service Support

From initial consultation and equipment selection through commissioning, training, maintenance, and eventual replacement — we provide end-to-end lifecycle management. 24/7 support hotlines and rapid field response keep your operations moving without interruption.

Pan-African Network

With service centers, warehouses, and technical teams deployed across 15+ African countries, we deliver localized support at continental scale. Proximity to your sites means faster response times, shorter lead times, and reduced logistics costs for parts and service.

Cost Optimization

Our flexible ownership models — purchase, lease, or rent — combined with predictive maintenance and telematics-driven fleet management help clients reduce total cost of ownership by up to 30%, freeing capital for core business investment.

Integrated Group Synergies

Leveraging Anago Group's mining, construction, agriculture, and industrial services divisions, we understand your operational needs firsthand — deploying equipment solutions born from real-world experience across Africa's most demanding sectors.
