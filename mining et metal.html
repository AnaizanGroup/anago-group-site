Africa's Mineral Wealth, Responsibly Extracted & Locally Transformed

Anago Group's Mining & Metals division is at the heart of our integrated industrial strategy. We operate across the full mining value chain — from geological exploration and resource assessment to extraction, processing, and international commodity trading.

Our approach prioritizes local transformation of raw materials, ensuring that Africa's natural resources generate maximum economic value within the continent. Every operation adheres to international ESG standards while creating meaningful employment and community development.

Our Value Proposition

What makes Anago Group's Mining & Metals division a trusted partner for governments, investors, and communities.

Deep Geological Expertise

Decades of combined geological knowledge and advanced survey techniques enable us to identify high-potential mineral deposits with precision.

Local Value Chain Optimization

We maximize in-country transformation of raw materials, creating local jobs, building processing infrastructure, and reducing dependence on raw mineral exports.

Environmental Stewardship

Rigorous environmental management plans, mine rehabilitation programs, and sustainable practices that protect ecosystems while extracting resources.

Community Partnership

Every mining project includes social development programs, local hiring commitments, and infrastructure investments that benefit surrounding communities.

International Trading Networks

Established commodity trading relationships with global markets, ensuring competitive pricing and reliable delivery of refined minerals and metals.

Regulatory Compliance

Full compliance with national mining codes, international standards (ISO), and conflict-free mineral certification across all jurisdictions.
