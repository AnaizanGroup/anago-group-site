Developing Modern & Sustainable Agriculture Across Africa

Anago Group's Agriculture & Livestock division is at the heart of Africa's food revolution. We develop and operate modern agricultural value chains — from large-scale crop cultivation and livestock farming to agro-industrial processing and distribution — creating integrated systems that feed communities and fuel economies.

Our approach combines traditional African farming wisdom with cutting-edge agricultural technology, precision irrigation, and sustainable land management practices. Every project is designed to strengthen food sovereignty, generate lasting rural employment, and build competitive local industries that can serve both domestic and international markets.

Our Value Proposition

Why communities, governments, and investors trust Anago Group to build Africa's agricultural future.

Rural Employment Creation

Our agricultural operations create thousands of direct and indirect jobs in rural communities — from farm workers and technicians to logistics operators and processing plant staff — transforming local economies and reducing urban migration pressures.

Sustainable Production

Climate-smart agriculture, organic farming practices, water-efficient irrigation, and soil conservation techniques ensure long-term productivity without depleting natural resources. We farm for today and protect the land for tomorrow.

Local Competitiveness

By building complete value chains from field to market, we reduce import dependency, lower food costs for consumers, and create African agricultural brands capable of competing on regional and international markets.

Food Security & Sovereignty

Every operation strengthens Africa's food sovereignty — reducing dependence on imports, building strategic grain reserves, developing drought-resistant crops, and ensuring stable food supply chains that protect populations during crises.

Smallholder Empowerment

We partner with smallholder farmers through outgrower schemes, cooperative models, training programs, and market access platforms — amplifying their productivity while ensuring they share in the value created across the chain.

Integrated Group Synergies

Leveraging Anago Group's construction, equipment supply, and industrial services divisions to build farming infrastructure, deploy agricultural machinery, and manage supply chains — creating efficiencies unavailable to standalone operators.
